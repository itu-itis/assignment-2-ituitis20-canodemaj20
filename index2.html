Sports

Sports and physical activities hold a very important position at our school,that is why a big amount of attention goes toward these activities. Throughout the years our shcool has had some of the most talented players in all different kinds of sport. From ping-pong(table tennis) to voleyball and football, Ali Demi High School has one of the most diverse and well designed Physical Education curriculums. Both teachers and students show a huge level of passion and hard work. Ali Demi prides itself in its achievements which are well deserved. We believe that sport and physical acitvity is very important for the body as well as for the students mental and psychological health


Cultural Activities

Ali Demi High School amongst other achivements and successes has a rich portfolio of cultural activities. Our school has given the students the possibility to express themseselves in variety of ways. Through different activities we have mangaed to bring out the best in our students and their talents. For example the book club is a very successful activity which consists of interested students and literature teachers but not only. These students even managed to win a national competition about the famous V.Hugho book "Les Miserables". Another form that our students express themselves is through theatre. We have one of the most talented group of young actors who have won national prizes and awards as well. Besides these we hold activities very often regarding different events and causes such as "The European Day of Languages", "The National Day of Albanian Language", Ali Demi "Workshop", "National Day of Risk Reduction from Natural Disasters" and a lot more.
Furthermore Ali Demi students have participated in first aid sessions held by The Red Cross Vlora. A lot of our students volunteer there representing themselves and their school making us proud.


School Trips

We believe that students and faculty members as well deserve the opportunity to sometimes take the lesson outside of the class. That is why here we also put a lot of effort in our school trips. They are a great way for students to blow off some steam and have a bit of fun with their peers but also a great way for an alternate education. School trips are held around Albania as well as outside of the cuntry. One of our greatest school trips was in Auschwitz, Poland on the 27th of January which is the "International Day of Commemoration in memory of the victims of the Holocaust ". During our visit we caught a glimpse of what was once one the most horrible buildings in the world. It was very educataionl for the students. They had the possibility to see what had happened with their own eyes and to get a better perception of what these people had to go through. When the students came back a few days later they held an exhibiton showing different photos, posters and displaying information about the tragedy of the Holocast. One of our students managed to capture this trip in the form of a video. Clik here... to watch this amazing and creative video.

Our History

On October 20, 1947, when Albania had just been liberated, the first general high school in history opened its doors in Vlora. of this city. "Ali Demi" high school of Vlora, one of the most important high schools from the '50s until today which is considered a cultural monument of national education. The high school was opened in a two-storey house near the old church Catholic.In 1969 he finally settled in the building that is still in the city center today. The first director it was Koço Papaproko who taught geography and history. In the history of "Ali Demi" high school, the names of the directors Koço Papaproko, Harilla Kuçuli, Ali Belshi, Eneida Vera et al. And Roland Dervishaj (current director) who, although with very different but virtuous features, for more than seven decades have given a dignified direction to the teaching and educational work of generations, This collective of excellent pedagogy has turned the school into the most beloved temple of knowledge that Vlora has had. Boundless worship and gratitude today is dedicated to the excellent teachers who laid the first stone of teaching in the high school "Ali Demi", such as "Teachers of the People" or those pedagogues who became the symbol of eternal of this enlightened school, as the "Deserved Teachers." Students from all over the city of Vlora were enrolled in the “Ali Demi” high school. From 1950 to 1990 they were educated in it a total of 3388 students. The extraterrestrial world of alidemas was filled with a long series of activities. in '60s and '70s they received a series of first prizes in the theater high school olympiads of Albania and published then some almanacs with literary creations. The pedagogical staff of the high school "Ali Demi" Sava in December 1990 received the credit historical that was among the rare collectives in Albania that with a special message welcomed the arrival of pluralism political in our country.In addition to the glorious history of the high school, there is also a large number of former students of the high school "Ali Demi", who became excellent students, who were and are prominent figures of the academic world in Albania and in the world, such as: Bardhyl Golemi, Emin Lafe, Ethem Lika, Aurela Pasku, Agim Muço, Vesel Peçuli, Arta Balili, etc. But this epochal education center in Vlora should have its most natural pride for that group of former students of who later became politicians and gave and give a valuable contribution to the entire state system of Albania, such as: Abedin Elmazi, Luiza Hoxha, Koço Kokëdhima etc. Equally honored should be felt nowadays Vlora public opinion with those talented former students of the high school "Ali Demi ”, who excelled as personalities with their achievements in the history of Albanian sports, such as: Mexhit Haxhiu, Aferdita Aliaj, Kastriot Malaj, Ilir Kallfa etc. This high school has been a "combine" of winners of national olympiads as well as international competitions of new talents in all fields of science.
